Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • White or Brown Rice
  • Egg
  • Meat Choice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegetable Choice

Cooking Steps

  1. Rinse rice grain thouroughly for a few minute under cool water
  2. Place rice in a pot with an 2:1 ratio of water to rice
  3. Set rice cooking pot flame to medium-high heat until water is boiled. Then put a lid on the pot while setting the flame to low-heat. Allow rice to cook until water inside the pot is completely cooked off.
  4. Chop vegetable and meat to even pieces
  5. In another pan, cook the meat and vegetable.
  6. When the rice is done cooking, add it to the meat and vegetable pan. Then add egg with soy sauce. Make sure to constantly stir to evenly distribute the food content
  7. When the rice is done, it is time to serve! Enjoy :)
